A foldable tree visualization of a Formance ledger **Chart of Accounts** schema. Renders nested account segments with ASCII connectors (`├─` / `└─`) and a dual-icon gutter: a folder marks a namespace, a coin marks a postable account, and a node with `.self` shows both. Parameterized `$segments` get a gold `{}` marker, and regex `.pattern` validators and `.metadata` defaults render as badges. Folders can be collapsed; pass `legend` to show the icon key.
