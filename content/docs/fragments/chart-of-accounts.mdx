A foldable tree visualization of a Formance ledger **Chart of Accounts** schema. Renders nested account segments with ASCII connectors (`├─` / `└─`), shows `.self` accounts, regex `.pattern` validators, and `.metadata` defaults as badges. Each branch with children gets a chevron and can be collapsed.
